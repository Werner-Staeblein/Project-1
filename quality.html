Our quality promise

We care about the environment

We care about the environment. Sustainability, environment protection and conservation of resources are increasingly important in new house building.

Sustainable construction minimizes the consumption of energy and resources in all phases of a building's lifecycle.

Green buildings go one step further by incorporating a clear focus on environmental protection in the planning and construction process. This includes environmentally friendly building materials together with energy-efficient construction methods.

Energy efficiency for sustainable living

Embarking on a journey towards sustainability we understand that energy conservation is paramount. At Homeland, we wholeheartedly embrace the pivotal role that energy efficiency plays in both environmental protection and long-term cost savings. Our commitment extends beyond conventional practices, as we integrate cutting-edge technologies to ensure our newly constructed houses stand as beacons of innovation and responsibility.

From the radiant glow of solar systems harnessing the power of the sun to the eco-friendly warmth generated by our advanced heat pumps, every element of our construction is meticulously designed with energy efficiency in mind. We prioritize controlled ventilation systems that not only optimize indoor air quality but also contribute to a reduced carbon footprint.

Surveillance by independent architects

At Homeland Real Estate, we believe in setting unparalleled standards in construction excellence. Throughout the entire construction phase of our new developments, we go above and beyond to guarantee quality and customer satisfaction. To ensure the highest standards are met, each project is meticulously monitored by an independent, commissioned assessor. This dedicated professional oversees every aspect of the construction process, ensuring that our commitment to superior craftsmanship is consistently upheld.

Our proven inspection concept is designed to instill confidence in your decision to choose Homeland Real Estate. This transparent and robust approach ensures that every detail is scrutinized, from the foundation to the finishing touches.
